Online Registration (PTC-COMET)

Freshmen Requirements

1. OFFICE OF THE COLLEGE REGISTRAR

  • Original Copy of Grade 12 Form 138 (Report Card)
  • 2 pcs photocopy of Grade 12 Form 138 (Report Card)
  • Original copy PSA Birth Certificate / Marriage Certificate for married women students
  • 2 pcs 2x2 ID Picture, white background and with name tag
  • Students will be advised of the releasing schedule of the Certificate of Registration (COR)

2. BUDGET AND ASSESSMENT OFFICE (Ground Floor)

  • Your fees will be assessed, and you will be provided with an Assessment Form.
  • Tuition fees for paying programs are based on per course unit and other applicable fees.

3. CASH OFFICE

  • Pay your tuition and other fees based on the assessment. UNIFAST programs are FREE but with an LMS Fee of P300 and CTC fee of P100 applicable to all programs.
  • Obtain an official receipt for your payment.

4. MANAGEMENT INFORMATION SYSTEM OFFICE

  • Obtain your PTC institutional email.
  • Have your School ID printed..

How To Enroll

01

Online Pre-Registration: Fill out the PTC-COMET form online.

02

Document Submission: Submit physical copies to the Registrar's Office.

03

Evaluation: Wait for the Dean's interview and document checking.

04

Official Enrollment: Receive your Certificate of Registration (COR).
